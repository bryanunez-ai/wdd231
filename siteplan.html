Mérida Chamber of Commerce

Website Site Plan — WDD 231 Group Project

Site Name

Mérida Chamber of Commerce

Domain availability

The name reflects the capital city of Yucatán, México. Suggested domains: meridachamber.mx or camaramerida.org. The short, recognizable name strengthens local identity and search relevance.

Site Purpose

This website will serve as a resource hub for businesses in the city of Mérida, Yucatán. The site will provide information on local events, host networking opportunities, create business directories, and encourage local shopping. It aims to promote local economic growth, support local businesses, and foster a strong sense of community among the merchants and entrepreneurs of the region.

Target Market

Business owners, entrepreneurs, and patrons in the city of Mérida and the surrounding Yucatán Peninsula, as well as tourists and new residents interested in supporting and connecting with local commerce.

Site Goals

  • Improve member engagement by providing valuable resources and networking opportunities.
  • Attract new businesses and encourage economic development in the community.
  • Improve the visibility and reputation of the chamber as a trusted authority in local business matters.
  • Promote local tourism and "shop local" initiatives that benefit Mérida's economy.

User Personas

Small Business Owner — María, 35

An entrepreneur who owns a small boutique in Mérida's Centro Histórico. She is looking for networking opportunities, business resources, and marketing support to help grow her shop.

Corporate Executive — Diego, 47

An executive at a large regional company seeking growth opportunities. His company is interested in partnerships, economic development resources, and networking with other business leaders in Yucatán.

The New Resident — Francisco, 29

Recently relocated to Mérida and eager to learn about services in the area. He is interested in business directories, event calendars, and community service opportunities.

Scenarios

"I own a local business and want to network with other owners. How do I join the chamber, and what are the membership benefits and fees?"

"What upcoming events and workshops are hosted by the chamber, and how can I register for the ones that interest me?"

"I'm considering relocating my business to Mérida. What is the local business environment like, and what companies already operate here?"

SEO Plan

  • Use relevant keywords (e.g., "Mérida business directory", "Yucatán chamber of commerce") in descriptions, content, and blog posts.
  • Verify the site on Google Business Profile.
  • Get inbound links / backlinks from member businesses to improve rankings.
  • Embed Google Analytics on all site pages to track engagement.
  • Add descriptive alt text and structured data for local business listings.
